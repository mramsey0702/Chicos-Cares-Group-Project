Empowering Women. Hearts. Hands. Home.

Chico’s FAS, Inc. is proud to partner with Habitat for Humanity® and share in their vision of a world where everyone has a decent place to call home. Our partnership started this Spring but you can continue to support by making a donation to Habitat today. To date Chico’s, White House Black Market and Soma along with our generous customers have helped raise more than $960,000. Through shelter, we empower women and their families to achieve their dreams of a better life.

Empowering Women Starts With You

Habitat for Humanity began in 1976 and has since grown to become a leading global nonprofit organization active in more than 1,300 communities throughout the U.S. and in more than 70 countries. Through financial support and volunteering, everyone can help families achieve the strength, stability and self-reliance they need to build better lives for themselves. Through shelter, we empower.

VIEW MORE

About Our Partnership

Since 2001, Chico’s FAS has partnered with Habitat for Humanity in Southwest Florida where our Company was founded and is currently based. To date, through the support of Women Build, countless volunteer hours and financial contributions, we've built 44 homes to help local families. In 2018, we are proud to expand this partnership by becoming a national sponsor of Women Build and joining Habitat's Home is the Key Campaign. As part of this sponsorship, Chico's FAS is committed to impacting 77 local communities across the country through a dedicated grant.
