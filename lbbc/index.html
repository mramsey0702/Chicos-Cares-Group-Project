Breast Cancer Touches Us All.

Chico’s FAS, Inc. and it's family of brands - Chico's®, White House Black Market®, and Soma® are proud to partner with Living Beyond Breast Cancer and share in their vision of a world where no one impacted by breast cancer feels uninformed or alone. Their mission is one of hope - connecting people with the most trusted breast cancer information and community of true support during all stages of the journey. This Fall, you can help make a difference for a woman you know by donating in any of our boutiques or online below. Learn more. Get Support. Be Empowered.

Did you know?*

  • This year, over 266,000 Americans will be diagnosed with breast cancer, and more than 40,000 will die of the disease.
  • One in 8 women will be diagnosed with breast cancer in their lifetime.
  • Breast cancer is the most commonly diagnosed cancer among women after skin cancer, representing about 30% of new cancer diagnoses.
  • 84 cents of every dollar donated to LBBC goes directly into programming and resources for those impacted by breast cancer.

*Source: LBBC.ORG

Support Starts With You

Over the past 27 years, Living Beyond Breast Cancer has earned a reputation within the medical community, media and public as one of the leading national organizations providing support, information and programing for all people affected by breast cancer regardless of age, stage of disease or financial circumstance. More than 500,000 people viewed their resources online last year alone. Living Beyond Breast Cancer understands the magnitude of a breast cancer diagnosis. As a community that has stood where you stand, LBBC understands what you feel and what you need to know. Living Beyond Breast Cancer provides the information you need and the community you want after a breast cancer diagnosis. Learn more. Get Support. Be Empowered.

About Our Partnership

Since 2004, Chico’s FAS has partnered with Living Beyond Breast Cancer. Our Company is their largest corporate partner, and to date, our brands have helped raise nearly $8 million in support of connecting women diagnosed with breast cancer with the most trusted information and a community of support. Chico’s FAS is committed to women’s health and well-being and empowerment. With the right resources, the journey through breast cancer doesn’t have to be a solitary fight. As Chico’s FAS’ commitment to LBBC has grown, so has LBBC – helping them reach more than one million women and their families, ensuring that no one has to face a breast cancer diagnosis uninformed or alone.
