Women Build:
Our Habitat for Humanity Story

The 2018 national partnership between Chico’s FAS and Habitat for Humanity grew from a 17-year local relationship in our founders’ backyard. Sharing the belief that women coming together can make a difference, Chico’s FAS Associates participate each year in Lee and Hendry County Habitat for Humanity women-led projects to build strength, stability and self-reliance through affordable housing in Southwest Florida. Since 2001, Chico’s FAS has sponsored and helped build 44 homes, gifting over $2 million and volunteering over 7,500 woman (and man) hours.

In 2018, Chico’s FAS is proud to be national sponsor of Habitat for Humanity’s Women Build program, a participant in Habitat's Home is the Key awareness campaign and sponsor of a dedicated grant expanding empowerment through shelter to 77 U.S. communities.

Chico’s FAS associates in Southwest Florida will celebrate the partnership in May 2018 by volunteering to build a 42nd home. Our blueprint for success is rooted in our vibrant Associate spirit - climbing ladders and working together to change the lives of families in need of a place to call home.

Key Milestones in the Chico’s FAS Habitat Story

2001

The partnership begins with Habitat for Humanity Lee and Hendry counties. Chico’s FAS makes a commitment to help make the dream of home ownership a reality for families by sponsoring 5 homes.

2006

Chico’s FAS celebrates its 25th home completed in Lee County with a renewed commitment to build three more homes the following year.

2011

Chico’s FAS becomes the title sponsor of Women Build in Lee & Hendry counties, committing to providing the volunteer hours and financing to help renovate homes each May in a two week building blitz with a home dedication the weekend of Mother’s Day.

2017

The 41st Chico’s FAS home is built in Lee County, providing a safe place for a single mother and her children. It is also the 1,500th home dedicated in Lee and Hendry. To date more than 3,500 people in SWFL now have a safe and affordable home through the work of our Company and so many others in the community.
