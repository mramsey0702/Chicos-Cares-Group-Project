Chico’s FAS Cares

Chico’s FAS and our family of brands, Chico’s, White House Black Market and Soma, are committed to positively impacting our customers, associates and the communities in which we live and do business. From our inception, giving back has been at the heart of our Company.

Our brand and corporate cause-related initiatives are focused on women’s health, well-being and empowerment. Our effort includes raising awareness and funds through local and national partnerships with organizations like Habitat for Humanity International, Living Beyond Breast Cancer, American Red Cross and the National Network to End Domestic Violence, as well as regional, community-based philanthropic causes, disaster relief and associate volunteerism.
