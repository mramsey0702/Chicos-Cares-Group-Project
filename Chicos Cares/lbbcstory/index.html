A Friend for the Journey – Chico’s FAS & Living Beyond Breast Cancer

Our Company has a vision of empowering women at every stage of life’s journey. For everyone affected by breast cancer, Living Beyond Breast Cancer (LBBC) is the best resource we know, providing people with trusted breast cancer information and a community of support. LBBC has connected countless people to the resources they need to transform the experience of a diagnosis into a journey of support, friendship and inspiration. White House Black Market began the friendship with their first campaign for LBBC in 2004. Since then our family of brands – Chico’s®, White House Black Market®, and Soma® alongside with you, our generous customers, have raised nearly $8 million for LBBC.

Chico’s FAS is the largest corporate partner for LBBC. The relationship has helped LBBC more than double, creating influential change in the lives of the more than one million people who use LBBC’s services and online resources.

Our commitment to the cause now incorporates a sponsorship of LBBC’s biggest annual events, conferences, seminars, webinars and the Reach & Raise movement.

Living Beyond Breast is not the cure but it is the solution when faced with the challenge of where to turn when you hear the words, “you have breast cancer.” It is a trusted friend, a library of curated resources from the best minds and clinics available and a source of HOPE. LBBC is empowerment - connecting you with the information you can trust and the support you can count on.

Key Milestones in the Chico’s FAS LBBC Story

2004

The partnership is born as White House Black Market launches it’s first limited edition necklace, giving back to Living Beyond Breast Cancer. A year later the WHBM Give Hope Collection is curated, becoming an annual tradition.

2009

Chico’s FAS donations hit the $1 million mark. In five years, Chico’s, White House Black Market and Soma become LBBC’s strongest corporate partners. The collaboration of resources allows LBBC to grow exponentially expanding their services to provide grants and opportunities for women to connect on their journey to live beyond breast cancer.

2010

The Forever 25 campaign receives national press on the CBS Early Show as White House Black Market celebrates it’s 25th Anniversary by featuring 25 breast cancer survivors in it’s Fall catalog and marketing. These women still gather today and have become a symbol of hope.

2015

Chico’s FAS brings LBBC’s yoga event - Reach & Raise – to Fort Myers, the corporate headquarters of the Company. The inaugural event is the most successful first time event for LBBC. Hundreds of people enjoyed an hour of yoga in the park while raising funds and awareness for breast cancer.
